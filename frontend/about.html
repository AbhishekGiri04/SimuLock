About SimuLock

Transforming Operating Systems Education Through Advanced Deadlock Simulation Technology

Our Vision

To establish the world's most advanced and intuitive deadlock simulation platform that revolutionizes operating systems education by transforming abstract theoretical concepts into immersive, interactive learning experiences accessible to students, researchers, and professionals globally.

Our Mission

Empowering the next generation of computer scientists, system architects, and software engineers with cutting-edge tools to master deadlock detection, prevention, and recovery mechanisms through real-time visualization, interactive simulations, and comprehensive analytical frameworks that bridge the gap between theory and practical implementation.

What is SimuLock?

Enterprise Simulation Platform

Industry-grade process and resource management simulation with advanced visual feedback systems, real-time deadlock detection capabilities, and comprehensive performance analytics for enterprise-level training and research applications.

Advanced Educational Framework

Comprehensive learning platform specifically engineered for computer science education, featuring progressive difficulty levels, guided tutorials, and adaptive learning pathways to master complex operating system concepts through immersive hands-on experience.

Intelligent Analytics Engine

State-of-the-art analytical framework providing comprehensive logging, advanced performance metrics, predictive modeling, behavioral pattern analysis, and detailed forensic examination of system behavior and deadlock formation patterns.

Core Features

Real-time Detection

Instant deadlock identification using advanced graph algorithms with visual alerts and detailed analysis.

Interactive Graphs

Dynamic wait-for graphs with real-time updates showing process-resource relationships and dependencies.

Auto Simulation

Pre-built scenarios and automatic deadlock creation for quick demonstration and learning purposes.

Live Monitoring

Real-time system status tracking with process states, resource allocation, and performance metrics.

Smart Logging

Comprehensive event logging with timestamps, categorized messages, and detailed system activity history.

Prevention Tools

Built-in deadlock prevention mechanisms and recovery strategies with educational explanations.
